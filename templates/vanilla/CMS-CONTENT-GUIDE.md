## 📊 Content Quality Standards

### E-E-A-T Optimization (Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, Trustworthiness)

#### Experience
- Real-world insights and practical examples
- Case studies from XXX_INDUSTRY
- Actionable advice based on actual implementation
- User success stories and testimonials

#### Expertise
- Government source citations for all claims
- Industry expert quotes and insights
- Data-backed recommendations
- Professional terminology used correctly

#### Authoritativeness
- Links to official government sites (.gov)
- Citations from academic institutions (.edu)
- References to industry bodies (.org)
- Partnerships with recognized experts

#### Trustworthiness
- Transparent about limitations
- Clear disclaimers where needed
- Regular content updates
- Correction policy for errors

### Citation Requirements

**MANDATORY for all content:**
- **Government Sources**: Always link to official XXX_GOVERNMENT_SITES
- **Financial Claims**: Cite official documents, fee schedules
- **Legal Information**: Reference current laws, regulations
- **Statistics**: Use current year data with source attribution
- **Processing Times**: Use official estimates with variability notes

**Minimum Citations by Stage:**
- Stage 1: 8+ authoritative external links
- Stage 2: 5+ authoritative external links
- Stage 3: 3+ authoritative external links

## ✍️ Tone & Voice

### Brand Voice for XXX_APP_NAME

#### Professional but Approachable
- **Expert without jargon**: Explain complex XXX_TOPICS in plain language
- **Confident but cautious**: State facts clearly, acknowledge changes
- **Helpful not salesy**: Focus on information, not promotion
- **Inclusive and respectful**: Consider diverse audiences

#### Writing Style Guidelines
- **Concise**: Get to the point quickly
- **Structured**: Use headers, bullets, tables for scanning
- **Action-oriented**: Tell readers exactly what to do
- **Visual**: Include tables, charts, infographics where helpful

### Example Tone:
❌ **Avoid**: "You absolutely must consider our amazing XXX_SERVICE!"
✅ **Use**: "XXX_SERVICE requires XXX_REQUIREMENT and provides XXX_BENEFIT."

## 🎯 SEO Optimization Standards

### Title Requirements
- **Length**: 50-60 characters (max 70)
- **Format**: Primary Keyword | Secondary | Brand
- **Example**: "XXX_KEYWORD Guide 2025 | XXX_APP_NAME"

### Meta Description Requirements
- **Length**: 150-160 characters (max 165)
- **Include**: Primary keyword, value prop, call-to-action
- **Example**: "Complete guide to XXX_KEYWORD. Learn XXX_BENEFIT with our expert insights. Updated 2025."

### URL Structure
- **Format**: /category/descriptive-slug
- **Length**: Keep under 60 characters
- **Example**: /guides/xxx-keyword-complete-guide

### Header Structure (H1-H6)
```
H1: One per page (title)
H2: Major sections (3-5 per article)
H3: Subsections (2-3 per H2)
H4: Details (as needed)
```

## 📊 Quality Scoring System (0-100)

### Scoring Algorithm

#### Content Depth (40 points max)
- 3000+ words: 40 points
- 2500-2999 words: 35 points
- 2000-2499 words: 30 points
- 1500-1999 words: 20 points
- 1000-1499 words: 10 points
- <1000 words: 5 points

#### Research Quality (25 points max)
- Tavily research used: +20 points
- 8+ external links: +5 points
- 5-7 external links: +3 points
- 1-4 external links: +1 point

#### AI Enhancement (20 points max)
- Critique Labs fact-checking: +10 points
- Firecrawl document parsing: +5 points
- LinkUp contextual search: +5 points

#### Currency/Freshness (15 points max)
- Current year data (2025): +15 points
- Last year data (2024): +10 points
- 2+ years old: +5 points
- No date references: 0 points

### Minimum Acceptable Scores
- **Stage 1 Content**: 85/100 minimum
- **Stage 2 Content**: 80/100 minimum
- **Stage 3 Content**: 75/100 minimum
- **Overall Average**: 80/100 minimum

### Update Priorities
1. Content with quality scores below 80
2. Content older than 6 months
3. Content with broken links
4. Content with outdated statistics

## 🚫 Content To Avoid

### Never Include:
- Unverified claims or statistics
- Internal links without MCP validation
- Outdated information (>2 years)
- Promotional language without value
- Duplicate content
- Thin content (<1000 words for main pages)
- Stock photos without relevance
- Keyword stuffing

## 💡 Best Practices

### For Maximum Impact:
1. **Start with user intent** - What problem are we solving?
2. **Use data and examples** - Support every claim
3. **Structure for scanning** - Most users scan, not read
4. **Include next steps** - What should the reader do now?
5. **Update regularly** - Fresh content ranks better
6. **Test and iterate** - Use analytics to improve
